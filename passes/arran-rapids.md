Arran Rapids runs off the west side of Stuart Island into Bute Inlet, part of the
rapids complex on the east route beyond Desolation Sound, and is long feared as
one of the most violent rapids on the coast.

Source confidence: medium — historical and secondary sources only. Exact peak
speeds (reported ~9 to 13 knots) and slack-window length are not primary-
confirmed. CHS Sailing Directions PAC202 (Discovery Passage to Queen Charlotte
Strait — not the non-existent "PAC205") is the volume for tabulated figures.
